Spinach and Ricotta Cannelloni

Cannelloni is a tube shaped dry pasta. It is stuffed with filling, covered in a sauce and cheese then baked. It does not need to be cooked before filling, it softens when baked in the oven. The Spinach and Ricotta Filling is creamy and extra tasty with the addition of parmesan and cheese. The ingredients are many but the recipe is very easy to prepare, make a large amount of tubes and then freeze them for later.

Ingredients

  • 18â€“22 dry cannelloni pasta tubes.
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella.
  • 250g chopped spinach.
  • 500g ricotta.
  • 1 cup shredded cheese.
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan.
  • 1 large chopped garlic clove.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 pasta sauce can.

Assembly:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Make the filling in a bowl, mix the chopped spinach (after we squeezed all the liquids out), ricotta, egg, shredded cheese, parmesan, and the garlic.
  3. Transfer the filling to a piping bag with a large nozzle (that fits in the tubes).
  4. Spread a bit of sauce on the base in baking pan.
  5. Pipe the filling into the tubes. place in baking pan, the extra tubes can be stored in the freezer.
  6. Pour over remaining Sauce, covering all the tubes, cover with foil, then bake for 25 minutes.
  7. Remove foil, scatter over Mozzarella cheese, return to oven for 10 minutes until cheese is melted.
  8. Serve with dino nuggets & enjoy.
